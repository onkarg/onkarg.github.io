About Me

Growing up I was endlessly intrigued by how everything around me worked. Even as a young child, if I saw something broken I felt driven to identify the problem and try to figure out a solution. The passion I've developed for programming has unfolded in my adult life in a similar way.

Recently I transitioned my career from being an Assistant Manager of a help desk team in Boston to following my dream of becoming a software engineer, learning to be able to have an idea and turn that idea into something tangible using React, Redux, HTML, CSS. After arriving in Phoenix I attended a bootcamp called DevMountain, which taught me the fundamentals of what it takes to become a software engineer, building projects on my own and with a team. I learned the importance of collaboration, teamwork to get more done, learning from others and sharing with others what I know.

Going through these projects has taught me to become a team player; has helped me to manage multiple tasks effectively and shown me the importance of version control. The passion I have developed for programming has driven me to tackle every problem with full force and to find its solution.
